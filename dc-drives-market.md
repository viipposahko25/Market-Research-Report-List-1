<p><p>The DC drives market comprises two primary segments: low voltage DC drives and medium voltage DC drives. Medium voltage DC drives, operating between 600 volts and 3,300 volts, are utilized in larger industrial applications, like mining and manufacturing, where higher power handling capabilities and enhanced durability are essential.</p></p>
