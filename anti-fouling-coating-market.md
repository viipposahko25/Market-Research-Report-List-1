<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anti-fouling coating market is primarily categorized into Type I and Type II coatings. Type I coatings utilize biocides to prevent the growth of marine organisms on surfaces, offering effective short-term protection. These coatings often require regular maintenance and reapplication. In contrast, Type II coatings use non-toxic, eco-friendly alternatives that rely on physical properties, such as surface texture, to deter fouling. While Type II products provide longer-lasting solutions with reduced environmental impact, they may be less effective in extreme fouling conditions.</p></p>
