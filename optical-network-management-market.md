<p><p>The Optical Network Management market encompasses various technologies, with Synchronous Optical Network (SONET) and Wavelength Division Multiplexing (WDM) being key types. SONET provides a standardized digital transmission hierarchy, enabling the efficient management of voice and data traffic over optical networks. In contrast, WDM enhances bandwidth by allowing multiple light wavelengths on a single fiber, increasing data transmission capacity. Both technologies facilitate network optimization and resilience, playing critical roles in modern telecommunications infrastructure and supporting growing data demands.</p></p>
