<p><strong>The Head-Up Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Windshield Projected HUD</li><li>Combiner Projected HUD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The head-up display (HUD) market is primarily divided into two types: Windshield Projected HUD and Combiner Projected HUD. Windshield Projected HUD projects essential information directly onto the vehicle's windshield, allowing drivers to access data without taking their eyes off the road. In contrast, Combiner Projected HUD uses a separate transparent screen, or combiner, to display information within the driver’s line of sight. Both types enhance driving safety and convenience by providing real-time data seamlessly integrated into the driving experience.</p></p>
