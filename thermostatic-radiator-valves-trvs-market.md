<p><p>Thermostatic Radiator Valves (TRVs) are devices that regulate the temperature in a heating system by controlling the flow of hot water to radiators. The market for TRVs is divided into two main types: self-operating TRVs and electronic TRVs. Self-operating TRVs use a mechanical mechanism to adjust the flow based on ambient temperature, offering simplicity and reliability. In contrast, electronic TRVs use sensors and programmable settings for more precise control, enhancing energy efficiency and comfort in heating systems.</p></p>
