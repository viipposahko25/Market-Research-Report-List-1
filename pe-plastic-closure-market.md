<p><ul><li>Compression Moulding</li><li>Injection Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PE plastic closure market consists of two primary manufacturing types: compression molding and injection molding. Compression molding involves shaping plastic by applying heat and pressure to a pre-formed pellet, resulting in strong, consistent closures commonly used for jars and bottles. In contrast, injection molding involves melting plastic and injecting it into molds, allowing for intricate designs and faster production rates, ideal for high-volume applications. Both methods cater to various industries, ensuring efficient, durable packaging solutions.</p></p>
