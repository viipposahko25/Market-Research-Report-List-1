<p><p>The Polyoxymethylene (POM) plastic market is divided into two main types: Homopolymer POM and Copolymer POM. Homopolymer POM, known for its high strength and rigidity, is commonly used in applications requiring superior dimensional stability and mechanical properties. In contrast, Copolymer POM offers enhanced toughness and resistance to environmental stress, making it suitable for more diverse applications, including those involving harsh conditions.
