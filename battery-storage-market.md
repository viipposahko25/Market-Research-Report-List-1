<p><p>The battery storage market can be categorized into two main types based on electric power phase: single-phase and three-phase systems. Single-phase electric power systems are typically used in residential applications, characterized by their simplicity and lower capacity, suitable for energy backup and small-scale renewable integration. In contrast, three-phase electric power systems are utilized in commercial and industrial settings, providing higher capacity and efficiency for larger installations, supporting grid stability, peak shaving, and renewable energy integration at scale.</p></p>
