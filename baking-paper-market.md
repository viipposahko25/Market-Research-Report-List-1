<p><p>The baking paper market is primarily categorized into two types: unbleached and bleached. Unbleached baking paper retains its natural brown color and is appreciated for being more eco-friendly, as it undergoes minimal processing. It is often favored for its sturdiness and resistance to heat. In contrast, bleached baking paper is treated to achieve a white appearance, offering a cleaner and more aesthetically pleasing option for presentation. Both types serve similar functions in baking and cooking, ensuring easy food release and facilitating cleanup.</p></p>
