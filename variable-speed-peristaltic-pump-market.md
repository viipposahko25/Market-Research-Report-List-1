<p><p>The Variable Speed Peristaltic Pump market is primarily categorized into two types: Peristaltic Tube Pumps and Peristaltic Hose Pumps. Peristaltic Tube Pumps utilize flexible tubing to move fluids, providing precise flow control and minimal contamination risk, making them ideal for laboratory and food applications. Conversely, Peristaltic Hose Pumps utilize robust hoses to handle thicker and abrasive fluids, suitable for industrial settings where durability is essential. Both types offer adjustable speeds, enhancing their versatility in various pumping applications.</p></p>
