<p><strong>The Titanium-based Alloys Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Alpha Titanium Alloy</li><li>Beta Titanium Alloy</li><li>Alpha + Beta Titanium Alloy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Titanium-based alloys are categorized into three main types: Alpha titanium alloys, Beta titanium alloys, and Alpha + Beta titanium alloys. Alpha alloys, composed mainly of alpha phase titanium, are known for high-temperature strength and corrosion resistance. Beta alloys, rich in beta phase, offer enhanced ductility and strength, ideal for high-stress applications. Alpha + Beta alloys combine the advantageous properties of both phases, providing a balance of strength and formability, making them suitable for diverse industries, including aerospace, medical, and automotive.</p></p>
