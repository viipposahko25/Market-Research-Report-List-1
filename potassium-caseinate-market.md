<p><ul><li>Sprayed Type</li><li>Extruded Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Potassium caseinate, a milk protein derivative, is available in two primary market types: sprayed and extruded. The sprayed type is produced by spray-drying a casein solution, resulting in a fine, soluble powder ideal for beverages, nutritional supplements, and dairy products due to its excellent emulsifying and stabilizing properties. The extruded type is created through extrusion processing, yielding a denser, granular form suitable for protein bars, snacks, and baked goods, offering enhanced texture and binding capabilities. Both types cater to diverse food and industrial applications.</p></p>
