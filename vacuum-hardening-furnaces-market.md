<p><strong>The Vacuum Hardening Furnaces Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oil Hardening</li><li>Gas Hardening</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vacuum hardening furnaces market encompasses two primary types: oil hardening and gas hardening. Oil hardening involves immersing heated metal parts in oil to achieve rapid cooling and enhanced hardness, making it ideal for high-strength applications. Conversely, gas hardening uses controlled gas atmospheres to optimize cooling rates, minimizing distortion while preserving part integrity. Both methods are essential for industries requiring precise heat treatment, offering advantages in efficiency, precision, and the ability to handle various materials.</p></p>
