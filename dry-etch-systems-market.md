<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Dry Etch Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reactive Ion Etching (RIE) Systems</li><li>Deep Reactive Ion Etching (DRIE) Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dry etch systems in the semiconductor industry primarily include Reactive Ion Etching (RIE) and Deep Reactive Ion Etching (DRIE) technologies. RIE systems utilize chemically reactive plasma to etch material selectively, allowing for high precision in creating intricate circuit patterns. In contrast, DRIE specializes in producing deep, high-aspect-ratio features, often used in MEMS applications, by alternating between etching and passivation steps. Both systems are essential for advanced semiconductor fabrication, enhancing device miniaturization and performance.</p></p>
