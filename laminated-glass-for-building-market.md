<p><p>Laminated glass for building applications consists of multiple layers bonded together to enhance safety, sound insulation, and UV protection. Key types include PVB (polyvinyl butyral), known for its strong bonding and durability; EVA (ethylene-vinyl acetate), which offers superior moisture resistance and transparency; and SGP (SentryGlass Plus), recognized for its high strength and impact resistance.
