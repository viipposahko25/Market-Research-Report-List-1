<p><p>The Wireless Broadband CPE (Customer Premises Equipment) market is divided into two primary types: indoor CPE and outdoor CPE. Indoor CPE devices, such as routers and modems, are designed for use within homes or offices, providing reliable internet connectivity in enclosed spaces. In contrast, outdoor CPE devices, including antennas and signal repeaters, are intended for external installation, enhancing coverage and signal strength over larger areas. Both types play essential roles in delivering robust wireless broadband services to users.</p></p>
