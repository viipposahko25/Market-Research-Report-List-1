<p><p>The foldable knives market is categorized mainly into two types: flat and jagged. Flat knives feature a smooth, straight edge suitable for precise cutting tasks, often preferred by professionals requiring clean slices. Jagged knives, on the other hand, have serrated edges, ideal for gripping and sawing through tougher materials. Each type caters to different user preferences and applications, with flat knives excelling in culinary and tactical use, while jagged knives are favored in outdoor and survival scenarios.</p></p>
