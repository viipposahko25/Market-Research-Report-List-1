<p><p>The thermal imaging fire detection system market is divided into two main types: active and passive systems. Active thermal imaging fire detection systems continuously monitor temperatures and detect anomalies in real-time, providing immediate alerts during fire incidents. In contrast, passive thermal imaging systems periodically capture thermal images, analyzing them for any significant temperature changes that may indicate a fire risk. Both systems enhance fire safety and prevention strategies but cater to different operational needs and environments.</p></p>
