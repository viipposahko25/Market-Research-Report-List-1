<p><strong>The Marker Pens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oily (Alcoholic) Marker</li><li>Water-based Marker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marker pen market can be categorized into two main types: oily (alcohol-based) markers and water-based markers. Oily markers utilize solvent-based ink, providing vibrant colors and quick-drying capabilities, making them ideal for non-porous surfaces like plastic and glass. They tend to be more permanent and water-resistant. In contrast, water-based markers use pigments suspended in water, offering a non-toxic option suitable for paper and textiles, with easier cleanup and environmental benefits, although they may not be as fade-resistant.</p></p>
