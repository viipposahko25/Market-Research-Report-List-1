<p><p>The laboratory drying ovens market is predominantly categorized into forced air and vacuum ovens, along with gravity convection ovens. Forced air ovens utilize fans to circulate heated air, ensuring uniform temperature distribution and faster drying times. Vacuum ovens operate under reduced pressure, allowing for efficient drying at lower temperatures, which is ideal for heat-sensitive materials. Gravity convection ovens rely on natural air circulation, providing a gentle drying process. Each type caters to specific laboratory needs, offering varying levels of efficiency and material compatibility.</p></p>
