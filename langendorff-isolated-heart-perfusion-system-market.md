<p><p>The Langendorff Isolated Heart Perfusion System market consists of two primary types: Constant Pressure and Constant Flow systems. Constant Pressure Langendorff systems maintain a fixed pressure throughout the perfusion process, ensuring consistent cardiac workload and simulating physiological conditions. In contrast, Constant Flow systems deliver a steady flow rate regardless of pressure fluctuations, allowing for variable perfusion pressures and enhanced adaptability in experimental settings. Both types are crucial for studying cardiac function and drug responses in isolated heart models.</p></p>
