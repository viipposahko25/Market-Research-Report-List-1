<p><p>The prostate cancer diagnosis and treatment market features key methods such as the Prostate Specific Antigen (PSA) test and the Digital Rectal Exam (DRE). The PSA test measures the level of prostate-specific antigen in the blood, aiding in early detection of cancer. Meanwhile, the DRE involves a physical examination of the prostate through the rectum, allowing physicians to check for abnormalities. Both methods are essential for initial assessment, guiding further diagnostic procedures and treatment decisions in prostate cancer management.</p></p>
