<p><p>The medical nonwoven adhesive tapes market features primarily two types: double-sided and single-sided tapes. Double-sided tapes possess adhesive on both sides, allowing for secure attachment between skin and medical devices or dressings, making them ideal for securing implants or wound dressings. In contrast, single-sided tapes have adhesive on one side, suitable for fixing bandages or surgical dressings to the skin. Each type serves specialized purposes in healthcare, enhancing patient comfort and ensuring effective wound management.</p></p>
