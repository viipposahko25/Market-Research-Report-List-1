<p><ul><li>Screw-in Cartridge Valve</li><li>Slip-in Cartridge Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cartridge valve market features two primary types: screw-in cartridge valves and slip-in cartridge valves. Screw-in cartridge valves are compact, easy to install, and designed for high-pressure applications, allowing for efficient flow control in hydraulic systems. Slip-in cartridge valves, on the other hand, are typically used in modular systems, providing flexibility in configuration and ease of maintenance. Both types enhance system performance and are essential components in various industries, including manufacturing, agriculture, and construction.</p></p>
