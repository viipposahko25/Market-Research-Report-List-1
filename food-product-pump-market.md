<p><p>The food product pump market is categorized into three main types: electric, pneumatic, and manual pumps. Electric pumps offer efficient and consistent operation, ideal for large-scale food processing. Pneumatic pumps utilize compressed air for fluid transfer, providing a hygienic solution with minimal risk of contamination, suitable for various food applications. Manual pumps require physical effort to operate, often used for smaller batches or in locations without power supply. Each type serves distinct needs in food production and distribution.</p></p>
