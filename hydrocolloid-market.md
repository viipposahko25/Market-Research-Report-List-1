<p><ul><li>Film Backing Hydrocolloid Dressing</li><li>Foam Backing Hydrocolloid Dressing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydrocolloid market is primarily segmented into two main types: film backing hydrocolloid dressings and foam backing hydrocolloid dressings. Film backing hydrocolloid dressings feature a thin, transparent film that provides a barrier against moisture and external contaminants, making them suitable for superficial wounds and sensitive areas. In contrast, foam backing hydrocolloid dressings incorporate a thicker foam layer, offering enhanced cushioning and moisture absorption for wounds with moderate to high exudate. Both types promote a moist healing environment and facilitate faster recovery.</p></p>
