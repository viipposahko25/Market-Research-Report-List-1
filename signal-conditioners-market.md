<p><p>Signal conditioners are essential devices that enhance the quality of signals for processing and transmission. The market is primarily divided into two types: analog and digital signal conditioners. Analog signal conditioners manipulate continuous signals to improve accuracy and reduce noise, catering to applications like temperature and pressure sensing. In contrast, digital signal conditioners convert analog signals into digital format, enabling advanced processing and integration with digital systems. Both types play a crucial role in various industrial, automotive, and communication sectors.</p></p>
