<p><p>The aerospace landing gear market is primarily segmented into main landing gear and nose/tail landing gear. Main landing gear is crucial for weight support during landing and take-off, ensuring stability and balance during ground operations. It typically comprises wheels and struts that absorb shock and facilitate movement. Conversely, nose landing gear is located at the front of an aircraft and supports the front weight, providing steering control, while tail landing gear, used primarily in certain designs, aids in balance and stability during ground maneuvers.</p></p>
