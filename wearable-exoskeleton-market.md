Lower wearable exoskeletons focus on assisting the legs and lower body, aiding in walking, lifting, and reducing strain during physical activities. Body wearable exoskeletons encompass full-body suits that provide comprehensive support for mobility and strength enhancement, benefiting sectors like healthcare, military, and manufacturing. Each type targets specific user needs and applications.</p></p>
