<p><p>The flying probe tester market is categorized into single-sided and dual-sided probing testers. Single-sided probing testers focus on accessing one side of a printed circuit board (PCB), making them ideal for simpler designs and cost-effective solutions. In contrast, dual-sided probing testers can access both sides of a PCB, allowing for comprehensive testing of more complex circuits.
