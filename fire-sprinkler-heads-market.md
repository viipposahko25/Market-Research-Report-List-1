<p><p>The fire sprinkler heads market comprises several types, including stent, glass bubbles, fusible alloy, and heat sink technologies. Stent sprinklers utilize flexible, spring-based mechanisms for activation, while glass bubbles contain liquid that expands and shatters under heat, triggering the sprinkler. Fusible alloy heads employ a low-melting-point metal that melts when heated, releasing water. Heat sink systems absorb heat, enhancing response time. Each type is designed for various applications, offering distinct advantages in fire suppression efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
