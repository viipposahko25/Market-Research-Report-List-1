<p><p>The styrene polymerization inhibitor market is divided into active and inactive segments. Inactive inhibitors, on the other hand, are substances that do not influence the polymerization process but serve to enhance safety during handling. Both types cater to different industry needs, with active inhibitors being critical for prolonging shelf life, while inactive inhibitors are used primarily for regulatory compliance and risk management.</p></p>
