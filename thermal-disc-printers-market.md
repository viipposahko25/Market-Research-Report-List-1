<p><strong>The Thermal Disc Printers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Thermal Printing</li><li>Thermal Retransfer & Dye-sublimation Printing</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Thermal Disc Printers market comprises various types of printing technologies. Direct thermal printing utilizes heat-sensitive media, producing images without ink, ideal for labels and shipping documents. Thermal retransfer involves printing on a film before transferring it to the media, offering higher quality and durability, suitable for ID cards and badges. Dye-sublimation printing uses heat to transfer dye onto surfaces, creating vibrant images, often used for textiles and photographic prints. Other markets may include specialized applications and emerging technologies.</p></p>
