<p><strong>The Agriculture Ventilation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Roof</li><li>Ridge</li><li>Floor-mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The agriculture ventilation system market includes roof, ridge, and floor-mounted types, each serving distinct needs. Roof ventilation systems promote efficient airflow by allowing heat and moisture to escape from the top, ideal for greenhouses and barns. Ridge vent systems are installed along the peak of structures, facilitating natural ventilation and reducing humidity. Floor-mounted systems circulate air close to the ground, effectively managing temperature and preventing stagnation. Each type enhances environmental control, supporting healthy plant growth and livestock wellbeing.</p></p>
