<p><p>The self-laminating labels market includes three primary types: polyester, polyimide, and acrylic. Polyester labels are durable, water-resistant, and suitable for various applications, making them popular for industrial and commercial use. Polyimide labels are heat-resistant, ideal for high-temperature environments like electronics and aerospace. Acrylic labels offer strong adhesive properties and clarity, often used for consumer products and signage. Each type caters to specific requirements in terms of durability, temperature resistance, and adhesion, fulfilling diverse labeling needs across industries.</p></p>
