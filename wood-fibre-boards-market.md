<p><p>The wood fibre boards market includes two primary types: porous wood fibre boards and hard wood fibre boards. Porous wood fibre boards are lightweight and offer excellent insulation and sound absorption properties, making them suitable for applications in packaging and building materials. Conversely, hard wood fibre boards, made from denser wood particles, provide enhanced structural strength and durability, often being used in furniture and flooring. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, emphasizing sustainability and environmental benefits in manufacturing.</p></p>
