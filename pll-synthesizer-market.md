<p><strong>The PLL Synthesizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Integer-N</li><li>Fractional-N</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PLL synthesizer market is categorized into Integer-N and Fractional-N types. Integer-N synthesizers generate output frequencies by locking to integer multiples of a reference frequency, ensuring stable and low-phase noise signals, ideal for applications requiring precision. Fractional-N synthesizers, on the other hand, offer finer frequency resolution by allowing fractional division ratios, enabling more flexible frequency generation. This versatility makes Fractional-N synthesizers suited for applications needing rapid frequency hopping and greater tuning range, appealing to modern communication systems.</p></p>
